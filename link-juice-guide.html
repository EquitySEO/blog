What is Link Juice?

Link Juice is an SEO term that refers to the value or authority passed from one page or site to another through hyperlinks. When one page links to another, it transfers a portion of its authority ‚Äî or ‚Äújuice‚Äù ‚Äî helping the target page rank higher in search engines.

How is Link Juice Transferred?

  • Through followed links (default link behavior).
  • From high-authority pages.
  • Via internal linking structure of your own site.

Factors That Affect Link Juice

  • Whether the link is nofollow or dofollow
  • Relevance of the linking content
  • Number of total outbound links on the page
  • Authority and trustworthiness of the linking site

Best Practices for Maximizing Link Juice

  • Use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text
  • Link from strong internal pages to new content
  • Minimize unnecessary nofollow usage
  • Regularly audit broken or low-quality backlinks
